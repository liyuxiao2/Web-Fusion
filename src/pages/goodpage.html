Good Web Design: Avenue2Learn

Visit Example Site

Why this is a good website: This has a unique, classy look. Interactive design, easy to navigate around the website. It has notifications, and assignments upcoming, and is accessible to students, and teachers. Easy to use for individual work, group work, assigning tests/quizzes, and assigning projects. It is mobile and responsive to phones, and tablets as well. On mobile, the website ensures ease of use by having padding around button links for bigger hitboxes when tapping. Avenue is a great example of fitting the needs of users without overengineering while still looking visually appealing. The feature of allowing a class owner to view the site as a student is a creative way to add instructive utility, which is just the tip of the iceberg of the various ways in which the site is designed and optimized for the role it is meant to fulfill.
