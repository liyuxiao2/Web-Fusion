TutorMatch

with Samuel Dovbenyuk

For our TutorMatch project, a collaborative effort by WebFusion, we are developing a platform that revolutionizes how tutors and tutees connect by implementing a swipe-based matching system similar to popular dating apps. Our client identified a gap in the market, noting that there are no platforms where tutors and tutees can connect with a simple swipe. In TutorMatch, tutors will create profiles by entering their qualifications, teaching style, and desired pay rate, while tutees will set their preferences to view tutor profiles one at a time via a dynamic search algorithm, swiping right to show interest or left to pass. Additionally, the platform will feature a dynamic rating and pay system, where tutors begin at a baseline rate that can increase based on positive feedback from tutees. We also plan to integrate AI to enhance the matching algorithm, ensuring more personalized and effective connections. Built using PHP, React, Node, Express, SQL, and JavaScript, TutorMatch is designed to be robust, scalable, and fully responsive, catering to both mobile and desktop users. Although we have yet to finalize specific design details, our goal is to develop an engaging, consistent, and user-friendly interface that presents essential information such as the platform name, credits to Sam and WebFusion, support contact details, and links to additional resources while remaining flexible enough to accommodate both mobile and desktop experiences.
